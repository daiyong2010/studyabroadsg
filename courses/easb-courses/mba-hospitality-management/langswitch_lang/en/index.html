MBA Hospitality Management

Course

MBA HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT
University Queen Margaret University Edinburgh UK
Course Objectives This course will attract managers in the hospitality industry who may wish to update their qualifications through consideration of contemporary issues or upgrade their qualifications, or managers looking for a career change, as well as graduates, with appropriate experience in the industry. It provides a solid base of theory, principles and practice of management relevant to the developing hospitality industry, whilst encouraging conceptual thinking and research ability.
Course Content 1. Marketing
2. Managing service industries, organizations and people
3. Operations management
4. Strategic management
5. Research methods for business
6. Independent Manager
7. Contemporary issues in Hospitality Management
8. Dissertation
Duration Typical delivery structure of Full-Time mode (15 - 18 months)
Course Fee Course Fees: SG$22,800.00
Examination Fees: S$1,800.00
Application and Enrolment: SG$300.00
Sponsorship: SG$300.00
Escrow Bank Charge/ Insurance Charge: SG$300.00
Total: SG$25,500.00
Admission Criteria Minimum 16 years old
‘A’ Levels or equivalent conducted with English
‘A’ Levels or equivalent conducted without English (Must do English Communication) Minimum 3 GCE ‘O’ Levels or equivalent with English (Must do CT and LS as enrichment modules)
Minimum 3 GCE ‘O’ Levels or equivalent without English (Must do English Communication, then Critical Thinking and Learning Skills as enrichment modules)
Additional Criteria The standard entry requirement is a UK honours degree plus management experience, or what is considered equivalent. Non-standard applicants will be considered if they can demonstrate that they have the necessary work-based experience, skills and aptitude to achieve the award.
Intakes Jan / April / July /Oct
