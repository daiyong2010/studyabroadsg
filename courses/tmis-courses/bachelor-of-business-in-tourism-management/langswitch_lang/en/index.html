Course

Bachelor of Business in Tourism Management
University

Southern Cross University is a dynamic, contemporary Australian University. It is proud of its reputation for quality teaching and the strength of its research achievements. It is established under its own Act of Parliament and is a full member of the 480 institution-strong global Association of Commonwealth Universities.

Graduates of this degree will have the management expertise to build a tourism and hospitality management career anywhere in the world. It places considerable emphasis on workplace experience graduation ceremony in SCU Australia or Singapore. This degree is recognised by government and professional organisations in most of the countries.
Course Overview The main emphasis of the course is to give students the best possible preparation for a successful career in tourism and hospitality management. Graduates will be suited to employment as professional managers in resorts and hotels, government and tourist organisations, clubs, airlines, tourist attractions and other special interest operations.
Course Content

Year 1 (Tourism Foundation Diploma) -- Introduction to Travel & Tourism, Visitor Attractions Management, Tourism Research, Travel Agency Operations, Economics, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Business Communication, Meetings, Incentives, Conventions & Exhibitions (MICE)


Year 2 & 3 (The Bachelor of Tourism Management) -- Introduction to Tourism & Hospitality Management, Business Law and Ethics for Tourism & Hospitality, Professional Development for the Workplace, Strategic Management for Tourism & Hospitality Enterprises, Conventions, Meetings and Exhibitions Management, Tourism Planning and the Environment, Entrepreneurship in Tourism & Hospitality, Gaming & Club Management, Tourism in Pacific Asia, Food & Beverage Management, Contemporary Tourism Issues, Intern Study I-IV / Research Project I-IV
Duration 3 Years
Course Fee S$31 779 = Year1 S$7 400 + Year 2&3 S$21 600 + Reg Fee S$700 + 7%GST S$2079
Admission Criteria Diploma: GCE ‘N’ Level Equivalent; PMR Qualifications
Bachelor: GCE 'O' Level Equivalent; (SPM) /STPM
(UEC) Qualifications; Diploma from a recognized educational institution
Intakes Jan, April, Jul, Oct
