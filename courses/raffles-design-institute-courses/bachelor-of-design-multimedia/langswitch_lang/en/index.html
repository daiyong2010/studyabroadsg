Bachelor of Design (Multimedia)

Course

Bachelor of Design (Multimedia)
University Raffles College of Design and Commerce, Sydney, Australia
Course Overview

The purpose of this programme is to equip students with the fundamental broad-based skills in graphic design, afterwhich, students learn to transform 2D graphics into the 3D world by creating interfaces and integrate programming abilities to illustrate the wonders of using multiple forms of Interactive Media.

Students are also presented with the opportunity to research multimedia design trends and issues within society to produce an integrated suite of design solutions, taking into account aspects of culture, economics and social interaction.
Course Content • Animator
• Art Director/Creative Director
• Multimedia Designer/Programmer
• Video or Film Production Artist
• Web Designer/ Developer
• Motion Graphics Artist
• Games Developer
• Video Installation Artist
• Graphic Designer
• Digital Media Artist
Duration 3 Years
Course Fee S$20 000/year + Application fee (S$1,500 + GST); Application fee is to be made payable with submission of Application documents. Application fee is non-refundable and non-transferable. Upon admissions to the programme, the $1,000 will be credited to the last installment. Course fee excludes materials and textbooks required for their area of study.
Admission Criteria Minimum 16 years old and have completed Senior High School equivalent (Malaysia - SMP, Indonesia - SMU, Myanmar - Standard 10, China - Senior High School).
ENGLISH LANGUAGE PROFICIENCY
TOEFL - minimum 550* (paper-based), 213* (computer-based) or 79-80 (Internet-based);
IELTS - minimum 6.0*; * Test result achieved not more than 2 years ago.
GCE 'O' Level English - minimum C6;
Advanced Level completion with Raffles Design Institute, Singapore
