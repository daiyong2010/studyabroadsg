Advanced Diploma in Game Design & Development

Course

Advanced Diploma in Game Design & Development
University ERC Institute
Course Objectives The course will provide participants opportunities to develop and demonstrate knowledge and understanding, skills, qualities and other attributes in the following:-Principles and practices within Computer Games Technology; Areas of Computer Games Technology and its applications; Range of tools and techniques used in the development of Computer Games Technology; Write critiques and develop a major piece of work in the Computer Games Technology; Current research issues in the relevant aspects of Computer Games Technology and Developed research methodology skills.
Course Content Module 1: Human Computer Interaction for Games
Module 2: Architecture & Design of Gaming
Module 3: Games Mechanics
Module 4: Game Design and Analysis
Module 5: The IQ of Serious Games
Module 6: The Business of Gaming
Module 7: OO Programming in C++
Module 8: Future of Digital Creativity & Multimedia
Module 9: Industrial Internship/Final Project
Final Project (for International Students)
Duration 12 Months
Course Fee SG$12,000 + Administrative fee of $100
Admission Criteria

ADVANCED LEVEL
Minimum 2 GCE "A" Level Passes (including at least a C6 for General Paper or C6 for English as first language for GCE "O" Level) with or without working experience
Applicant who is unable to demonstrate the above mentioned English proficiency is required to take our ERC Institute's English Placement Test


Applicants should possess either of the following(s):
Diploma in Game Design and Development awarded by ERC Institute
Higher Nitec (2-year duration) from the Institute of Technical Education) in one of the following disciplines: -Information Technology; - Wireless Technology
Candidates will be required to do Advanced Integrated Programme (AIP) if they possess Diploma in other principles
Game Design
Game Animation
Game Programming

Intakes Jan, April, July, Nov
