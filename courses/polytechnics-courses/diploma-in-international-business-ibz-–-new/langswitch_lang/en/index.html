Diploma in International Business (IBZ) – New!
Course Objectives The new Diploma in International Business aims to develop you into a young professional with a global and entrepreneurial mindset.
Armed with a firm foundation in business, first-hand international experiences and multilingual skills, you will be poised to manage complexities in a multicultural landscape, and succeed in an international marketplace.
You will also have the unique opportunity to expand your horizons with immersion programmes involving cross-cultural learning, exchange programmes or community service projects. Through these immersion programmes, you will develop the self-confidence, independence, tenacity and sensitivity needed to excel in different cultures and diverse business environments.
Course Content Year 1- Business Statistics, Business Communication, Business Management , IT in Business, Microeconomics, Creativity & Applied Thinking Skills, Sports & Wellness, Business Law, Decision Support with Spreadsheets, Macroeconomics, Accounting, Regional and World Affairs, Individual & the Community, Life Management Skills
Year 2- Business Communication, Cross Cultural Perspectives, International Business Environment, International Marketing, Service Operations Management, Any 2 Interdisciplinary Studies modules, Electronic Business Applications, Enterprise Creation & Development, International Human Resource Management, International Purchasing , International Trade and Practices, Innovation & Enterprise in Action
Year 3- Business Communication, International Finance, International Business Research, Global Supply Chain Management, World Issues: A Singapore Perspective, Any 1 Interdisciplinary Studies module, 6-Month Local or Overseas Industrial Attachment
Duration 3 Years
Course Fee $2000++ Per Year (After 80% Government Grant)
Admission Criteria GCE O-Level or equivalent or 'A' Level examinations
Intakes April
