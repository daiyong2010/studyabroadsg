Course

Bachelor of Environmental and Occupational, Health and Safety
University University of Newcastle, Australia
Course Overview

The FIRST bachelor degree programme in Singapore and in the region that encompasses both environmental and occupational health and safety (EOHS).

The modules offered in the programme range from science, laws and regulations, to management and practices. There exists a good balance of environmental, occupational health and safety and integrated EOHS courses. Students will gain knowledge to enable on-the-job application based on the tailored Singaporean content, as the curriculum embraces the content of the Safety Officer Training Course (Ministry of Manpower) and the Environmental Control Officer Course (Ministry of Environment) and local EOHS laws.

At the same time, comparative studies and appreciation of international issues are facilitated through the courses on international management practices and perspectives.

The University’s Accolades
• The University of Newcastle ranks among Australia's Top 10 Universities for research output and annual research income
• 4-star ratings in Graduate Starting Salaries, Toughness To Get In, Research Grants achieved (The Good Universities Guide 2007)
• All of the engineering degree courses offered are accredited by Engineers Australia (EA) and recognised by Washington Accord for entry to engineering practice.
• Accredited Service Provider for Safety Officer Training Course by Ministry of Manpower, Singapore

Course Content Bioscience for EOHS, Environmental Control Practice, OHS Practice, Occupational Health, Hygiene and Toxicology, Ergonomics for OHS, Occupational Safety, EOHS Management, Environmental Impact Assessment, Industrial Ecology for EOHS, Epidemiology & Environmental Health, Research Methods in EOHS, International Perspectives on EOHS, EOHS Law, Risk Assessment & Management.
Duration This course can be completed within a minimum period of 2 years
Entry Requirements The minimum requirement is a diploma in science or engineering from the local polytechnic institutions with average B/C grades.
Applicants without the required average grades may also apply but will be assessed on a case-by-case basis by the University.
Exemptions Applicants with the relevant qualifications average B/C grades are exempted from certain modules based on University’s assessment.
Intakes January, May every year
Course Fee Total course fee: S$21,186.00; Per module fee: S$1,324.13 All fees are inclusive of 7% GST but exclude textbooks.
