PSB Life Science Foundation
Course Overview Life Sciences is more than just test-tubes and men in white coats. It is a fascinating multidisciplinary field of study which involves exciting research, designing and executing groundbreaking experiments to perhaps develop new products (medicines, perfumes, even new types of foods) and processes. If you have imagined yourself as say, the discoverer of a cure for AIDS, then this course is the first step you should take to realising that dream. Don’t have a background in life sciences for the Bachelor of Science programme? This course bridges just that gap. It aims to: • Give a solid foundation in Chemistry, Biology and Maths to non life-science trained students wishing to make a career in life sciences and biotechnology • Support management professionals by giving them fundamental knowledge in this subject • Prepare students for new career opportunities in this area. This course also allows you to make a smooth transition to any of our many Psychology programmes.
Recognition Upon successful completion, graduates of this programme can advance on to a Bachelor of Science course offered by The University of Western Australia with an average 'C' grade, with a grade no lower than 'D' in any of the units. (subject to University’s entry requirements). Students must also meet the University's English Language Proficiency requirements.
Duration The course can be taken within a minimum period of 12 months.
Entry Requirements The minimum entry requirements are:
5 GCE ‘O' Level credits with ‘B4' average grade including English and Mathematics
Language Proficiency Minimum English proficiency requirements:
• C6 or better for GCE ‘O’ Level
• 570 (paper-based total) and 230 (computer-based total) in TOEFL with Essay Rating/TWE of 4.5 or Internet-based Test (iBT): An overall score of 90 with a minimum score of 22 in the Writing section and no less than 20 in the other sections
• 6.5 in IELTS with no band less than 6.0
• ‘C’ grade for the Cambridge Certificate of Proficiency in English
Others
GCE ‘A’ Level Mathematics or equivalent is also a pre-requisite for entry to the Bachelor of Science courses.
Intakes March, June, October every year
Course Fee Total course fee: S$2,214.90 (3 instalments) = S$6,644.70 All fees are inclusive of 7% GST but exclude textbooks.
