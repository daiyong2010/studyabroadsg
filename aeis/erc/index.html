Singapore Foreign Student Services Centre

 

ERC

ERC Institute is a leading education provider in Asia. We were founded by some of Asia’s top business leaders to groom the next generation of business captains. Our students enjoy excellent education and recreational facilities so that they can have an outstanding holistic education. They are also groomed and mentored by our panel of corporate titans to have that edge over their peers. Over 5,000 students have entrusted their education needs with us, so come join us so we can share our secrets of success with you.

ERC Institute has four key areas of specialisation. It carries the flag of the entrepreneurial spirit and has a key segment with its entrepreneurship studies, the three other key segments consist of Tourism and Hospitality Management, Finance and Investment as well as Digital Media and Information Technology. Excellence, Resolve and Cutting Edge (ERC) are the three strong values and characteristics of ERC Institute.

Vision

To be a premier institute providing tertiary business education worldwide.

Mission

We provide education with a difference. Our facilitators are committed to deliver quality and industry-relevant courses with excellent student services in conducive, state of-the-art facilities.

Values

Excellence • Resolve • Cutting-Edge
